ABOUT US!

Our mission at Fastner Swim School is to provide a high level of swim education through private swim lessons at an affordable price for all. We teach any age and have specialized instructors for each age group. Our instructors consist of veteran swimmers who have been trained by the best and are passionate about what they do. Where the swim lessons are conducted depend on what is most convenient for you. We are able to drive to your home to conduct the lessons or can find an alternate pool that works for everyone. Please read below to get more information about our instructors, our work, and our pricing. You can find our contact information at the bottom. Please dont hesitate to reach out to get started with swim lessons or if you have any questions. Thank You!

Safe

The top priority for us at Fastner Swim School is safety. We conduct all our lessons with care to prevent injury and train our swimmers to be safe in the water. All our instructors are COVID Vacinated!

Fun

We do our best to make each lessons fun for our swimmers to reward hard work in the water and to provide the best experience!

Fast

Our aim is to make better swimmers by educating on proper swimming techniques!
